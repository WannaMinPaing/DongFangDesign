DongFang Pay Partner

DongFang Pay Partner means the merchants who have a legal contract with DONGFANG IT SERVICES COMPANY LIMITED, the owner of the DongFang Pay application. Merchants have a separate contract to pay for the goods with the DongFang Pay application up to the specified amount. Customers can easily pay by scanning the Partner QR Code at these partner stores.

Product Features

Secure Money Transfer

Users can securely send and receive money as they can check the recipient's account and view the transfer records.

Top Up Easily

Top up and data package purchasing can be done easily with one click. Users can also view purchase records to avoid disputes.

Cash In & Out

You can easily deposit and withdraw money through DongFang Pay Center by meeting representative counter accounts in any currency.

Secure Transaction

For every transaction you must enter the correct passcode and then the backend will use another verification process, so it is completely secure.
